# 📊 E-commerce Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Overview
This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing e-commerce data to track business performance, customer behavior, and delivery efficiency.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Monitor revenue and order trends
- Analyze customer satisfaction and delivery performance
- Identify top-performing regions and product categories
- Understand payment method distribution

---

## 📊 Key Metrics
- Total Orders
- Total Revenue
- Late Delivery Rate (%)
- Average Delivery Time (Days)
- Average Customer Rating

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features
- 📍 Geographic distribution of orders
- 📉 Revenue and order trend over time
- 🏆 Top states and product categories
- 🍩 Revenue share by payment method

---

## 💡 Key Insights
- Revenue is concentrated in top-performing regions
- Delivery delays impact customer satisfaction
- Certain categories drive most of the revenue
- Payment methods show diverse customer behavior

---

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Power BI
- DAX
- Data Modeling

---

## 📂 Dataset
Brazilian E-commerce Public Dataset (Olist)
